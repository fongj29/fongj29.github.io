Dear Parents,

On Sunday, October 1st, my roommate, Kiki, and I went to the 2023 NYC Toy Fair. It wasn’t open to the public, and was actually geared more towards toy makers and distributors/wholesalers. I didn’t take as much pictures as I would since some businesses weren’t letting people take because of privacy. However, I noticed a lot of repeated ideas. So many plushies were starting to look like squishmallows or resemble food with faces. Boorrring.
